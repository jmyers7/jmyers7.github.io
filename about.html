I am a theoretical mathematician by training, with a Ph.D. in commutative ring theory and homological algebra.

This website is my attempt to break free from the insulated and siloed academic research environment that often demands hyper-specialization. I believe that my training in mathematics can be valuable in other areas of science and human knowledge, and I want to explore those possibilities. Through the posts on this website, I aim to share my journey of learning and discovery beyond the boundaries of my field, and to document the insights I gain along the way.
