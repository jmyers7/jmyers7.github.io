\prob A car rental company charges \$40 per day and 15 cents a mile for its cars.

\medskip
\begin{enumerate}
\item Write a formula for the cost, $C$, of renting a car as a function, $h$, of the number of days, $d$, and the number of miles driven, $m$.

\bigskip
\textcolor{red}{$C = h(d,m) = 40d + 0.15 m$}
\bigskip

\item Find $h(5,300)$ and interpret it.

\bigskip
\textcolor{red}{We have
	\[h(5,300) = 40\cdot 5 + 0.15 \cdot 300 = 245
	\]
This means that it cost \$245 to rent a car for 5 days, during which 300 miles are driven.}
\bigskip

\end{enumerate}
