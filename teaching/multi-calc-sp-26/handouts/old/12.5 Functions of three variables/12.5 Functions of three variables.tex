\prob The temperature, in $^\circ$C, at a point $(x,y,z)$ is given by $T(x,y,z) = x^2+y^2+z^2$. What do the level surfaces of $T$ look like and what do they mean in terms of temperature?

\medskip

\prob A company makes three sizes of cardboard boxes: small, medium, and large. It costs \$2.50 to make a small box, \$4.00 for a medium box, and \$4.50 for a large box. The fixed costs to make any number of boxes is \$8000.

\medskip
\begin{enumerate}
\item Express the cost, $C$, of making $x$ small boxes, $y$ medium boxes, and $z$ large boxes as a function $C = f(x,y,z)$.\vfill
\item Find $f(3000,5000,4000)$ and interpret it.
\end{enumerate}
